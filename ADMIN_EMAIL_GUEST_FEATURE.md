Subject: New Feature: Guest Requests Now Available

Hi team,

We've added the ability for members to request guests for our weekly games. Here's how it works:

**For members:**
When a member RSVPs "In," they'll see an option to request a guest on their RSVP page. They just need to provide the guest's name — email, phone, and GHIN are optional but helpful.

**For admins:**
When a guest request comes in, you'll receive an email with the guest's details and two buttons: Approve and Decline. Just tap one directly from the email — no need to log in. If another admin already handled it, you'll see a friendly "already handled" message.

When you approve a guest:
- The member (and guest, if their email was provided) get a confirmation email
- If the guest's GHIN number is missing, the email will prompt everyone to reply-all with it so the pro shop has it on file
- The guest will be included in the weekly confirmation and suggested groupings emails

When you decline a guest:
- Only the member and admins are notified — the guest is never emailed about a decline

You'll also get a reminder if any guest requests are still pending as the RSVP deadline approaches.
